CKS Memorial Hall

Building:
Visitors are struck by the magnificence of the lofty buildings and the pleasing scene of lush greenery and colorful flowers. The height is 70 meters. It is a great place for recreation and leisure.

History:
After Chiang, Kai-shek passed away, people started to find a place to have a building for him. It opened to the public on 1980.

Area:
It occupies 26 acers. Each hour, there is a guard exchange show.

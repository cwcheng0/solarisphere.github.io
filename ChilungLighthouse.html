Chilung Lighthouse

The Chilung Lighthouse, built in 1900, is located at the pier on the west bank of Keelung Harbor. It has been converted to Pier 30. Keelung Lighthouse also had a red light arc in the early years to mark the dangerous reefs outside the port. The red light arc was also removed due to the explosion of the dangerous reef.

If you want to go the Lighthouse, go from the Guanghua Road to the road that leads to the Baimiyu Fort Road and then go up about 20 meters. Parking is not easy, and the space in the hinterland is not enough. For large-scale development, it can only accommodate a few secret adventures. Chilung Lighthouse was opened on July 18 this year.

About Taipei 101
Taipei 101 was the tallest building in the world before 2008. It is still the tallest building in Taiwan.

About the office building
The Taipei 101 office building market is a top-class office environment. There are a number of architectural indicators in the vicinity of the Xinyi project. The average single-storey area is 800 square meters, with wide halls, displays and public spaces.

About the viewing platform
Visitors can purchase tickets at the ticket office on the 5th floor. They can enjoy multimedia videos in the queuing area and take the world's fastest elevator to the 89th floor observation deck. The two dedicated elevators on the observation deck can be reached in just 37 seconds at a speed of 1010 meters per minute.

About the shopping center
Taipei 101 MALL selects international fashion and exquisite food street, and the tangible space creates an infinite mood, making shopping a comfortable experience.

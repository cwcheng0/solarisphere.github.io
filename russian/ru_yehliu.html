Yehliu Geo Park

The rock landscape of Yehliu Geopark is one of most famous wonders in Taiwan and in the world. The costal line is stretching in a direction vertical to the layer and the structure line; crustal movement all contribute to the rare and stunning geological landscape. The total area is about 2 acers.

Three are 3 areas in this park. The first area is mushroom rock and ginger rock. The second area is similar to the first area, where the mushroom rock and the ginger rock are the main focuses. The third area is the wave-cut platform located on the other side of Yehliu.

Yehliu Geopark is famous for its sea-erosion landscape.

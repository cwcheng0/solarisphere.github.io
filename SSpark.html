The Songshan Cultural and Creative Park was originally a tabacoo factory. In 2011, it strived to be a hub for inspiring, nurturing the spirits of creativity and Taipei's creative hub. It is not a commercial place. Instead, it is a place for culture events, speeches, leasure activities, etc.

Trying to keep the original buildings and trees, it keeps the old houses modern design concepts. There are always culture events. People stay here during weekends to chat, have a cup of tea, and fool around all day.
